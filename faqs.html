Frequently Asked Questions

General

Who are you?

Lightspeed Global distributes plastic model kits, accessories, and collectibles.

Do you sell to the public? Who can register?

We only sell to dealers and retailers with physical brick-and-mortar stores. We do not sell to sellers who exclusively sell on third-party marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, or Walmart.

What is the minimum order?

The minimum order is $350.

What brands do you carry?

Please refer to our About page for a complete list of the brands we carry.

How do I see the prices?

To see our prices, log into the website. To request a login, please click on the Register button on the Login page.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Email Money Transfers (within Canada), and ACH Transfers (within the US).

Can I order replacement parts?

Please reach out to your retailer for parts requests. We no longer accept replacement part requests directly.

Do you have a warehouse in the USA and Canada?

Yes, we have a warehouse in Avondale, Arizona (USA) and Toronto, Ontario (Canada).

Shipping

Where do you ship?

We ship throughout Canada and the United States.

What are your shipping terms?

We strive to fulfill orders within 2 business days. If there are any issues, we will contact you.

How much do you charge for shipping?

We offer free shipping within the continental US and Canada for orders over $500. Orders under $500 are capped at 10% of the invoice total.

Parts were damaged during shipment. What can I do?

Contact us regarding the damaged items. Return the damaged products to our warehouse (Toronto or Buffalo), and we will issue a credit to your account.

I received the wrong product or something is missing.

Contact us at care@lsghobby.com if you received the wrong product or something is missing from your order.

Pre-order

What is a pre-order?

A pre-order allows you to reserve items before they arrive at our warehouse.

How to pre-order?

To pre-order, simply add the product marked as "pre-order" to your cart and proceed to checkout.

When will I receive my pre-ordered items?

We strive to ship pre-orders as soon as they arrive at our warehouse, usually on the product's scheduled release date.

When will I be charged?

You will be charged for pre-ordered items when they are ready to be shipped.

How do I cancel a pre-order?

Contact us by phone or email to cancel your pre-order.
