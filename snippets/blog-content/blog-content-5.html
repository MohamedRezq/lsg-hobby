News From Japan: MF Ghost

By Bill | May 15, 2024

Following on from - and in addition to - the Highly successful Initial D series, Our Man in Japan brings news of Aoshima's very newest of offerings: the ZN6 Toyota 86:

MF Ghost is a sequel to the much-loved Initial D, and is set in a near future where self-driving EVs have largely supplanted the old ICE technology. Street racers continue to compete with ICE cars however, and this is where the story begins.

This first upcoming release from Aoshima is of the car driven by the series' protagonist, one Kanata Katagiri. Fair play - this does look pretty cool!
