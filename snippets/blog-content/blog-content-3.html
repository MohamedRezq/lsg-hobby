D-Day 80th Anniversary is coming!

By Bill | May 22, 2024

As most of us probably know, it's the eightieth anniversary of D-Day this year; on June 6th, it'll be eight decades since the largest seaborne invasion in human history.

Interest in the anniversary and the associated history is likely to peak next month, and we wanted to remind everyone to ensure you have suitable stock in place to cater to your customers' needs.

For example, pre-orders are still being accepted for Eduard's D-Day Mustangs set, and the ever-popular Spitfire, represented by their new model kit of the Mk IXc. Don't paint your invasion stripes too precisely though - most of the real ones were pretty rough and ready...

On the vehicle side, we've got a couple of promising DUKW model kits incoming, although - if pressed - I might agree that the palm trees on the box art aren't *exactly* characteristic of the Normandy flora (I checked)!

D-Day also included a spectacular naval force, including many icons of the era; to pick a couple of examples, we have HMS Rodney (or 'Rodnol' if you agree with those who unkindly declared that she looked like an oil tanker!), and HMS Ajax (1939 configuration, veteran of the River Plate).

So, heads-up: the anniversary is nearing! Our friendly Sales guys are poised to take your call if you have need of guidance.
